Delicately Designed

Hello Darling,

My name is Ashton Michelle Spann, and I am a Sophomore at Miami University. I have been accepted, along with nineteen other students, into the Graphic Design program in the School of Creative Arts due to a successful portfolio review and interview. My expected graduation date is May 2016, and I will graduate with a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Entrepreneurship.

In high school, I was enrolled in several art courses including courses specialized in computer graphics, drawing/painting, and sevral aspects of three-dimensional art. Through these classes I was able to create a successful portfolio that enabled my acceptance to the colleges of my choosing. Without the help of my high school art teachers, Craig Huffman and Matt Young, I would not be where I am today.

My college experience within the School of Creative Arts has greatly improved my skills not only as a designer, but as an artist as well. Through my riggorus course work, I have been able to challenge myself and question the ordinary.
